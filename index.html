About the Human Heart

The human heart is a muscular organ that works as a pump. It circulates blood throughout the body to supply oxygen and nutrients and to remove waste materials.

The heart is about the size of a closed fist and is located slightly towards the left side of the chest, between the lungs.

Heart Attack

A heart attack happens when the blood flow to a part of the heart muscle is suddenly blocked. Because the heart does not get enough oxygen, the muscle begins to get damaged or die if treatment is delayed.
